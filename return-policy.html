Returns & Refunds

How we handle order concerns

Draft policy prepared for review. Product-specific return eligibility should be confirmed before launch, especially for food, chilled, or perishable items.

Return Eligibility

Because CaneCreme sells food and dessert products, returns may not be accepted once an item has been opened, used, damaged after delivery, or handled in a way that affects safety or quality.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you receive a damaged, missing, or incorrect item, please contact CaneCreme as soon as possible with your order details and clear photos of the product and packaging.

Refund Review

Refunds or replacements will be reviewed case by case. If approved, the refund will be processed to the original online payment method through the payment provider.

Cancellations

Cancellation requests can be made before the order is packed or dispatched. Once an order has moved into fulfilment, cancellation may not be possible.

Support
